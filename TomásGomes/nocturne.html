The Mission

The main objective was to architect a user interface that remained unobtrusive during intense combat while providing vital information—such as health, stamina, and ability cooldowns—instantly and clearly.

Note: In this project, I acted as the UI/UX Architect. While the final visual assets were produced by a dedicated artist, I was responsible for the strategic placement, visual hierarchy, and the overall functionality of every element in the HUD.

User Experience Strategy

To ensure player focus, I implemented a minimal HUD approach where high-priority bars are centered but transparent enough to not block enemies. Secondary menus were designed with a "nested" logic, allowing players to navigate complex inventory systems with minimal clicks.
