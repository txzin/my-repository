The Concept

Cyber Engineer is a game project where the intersection of different info visualization and futuristic aesthetics had to work well together, since the game is designed for long duration rounds. The goal was to design a Heads-Up Display (HUD) that feels advanced and "high-tech" while maintaining absolute clarity for the user.

Challenge

Designing for a Sci-Fi setting often leads to "visual noise". My approach here was to use the right colors and dynamic scaling to ensure that the right information had the user's attention.
